Our Privacy Policies

Welcome to Niche Security Services ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard personal information related to our security guard jobs and services. By interacting with our website and services, you agree to the practices outlined below.

We collect personal information from applicants and job applicants, including contact information, employment history, and other relevant information.

The use of information

The personal information collected is used to facilitate job applications, match qualified candidates with security roles, provide security services to customers, and improve our product offerings.

Sharing information

We may only disclose personal information to customers, partners and service providers to meet security and service requirements.

Data security

We use reasonable measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.
